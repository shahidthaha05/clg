WELCOME TO GPTC MUTTOM



The Government Polytechnic College, Muttom was established in 1981 under the Department of Technical Education, Kerala State. It is a leading technical institution in Idukki district and serves as the nodal polytechnic for the region .

Here are some highlights about the college:

Courses:

The college offers diploma courses in five branches:
1.Civil Engineering,
2.Mechanical Engineering,
3.Electrical and Electronics Engineering,
4.Electronics Engineering,
5.Computer Engineering.


Faculty:
The college has highly educated faculty members

Fees:
The college offers courses at affordable fees.

Accessibility:
The college has a wheelchair accessible entrance and parking lot.

Location:
The college is located in the rural area of Muttom, which is part of the Midland or Idanad region of Kerala.
It is 8.5 km away from Thodupuzha and 66 km away from Kochi.
